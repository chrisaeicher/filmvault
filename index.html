The idea

A home for your
cinematic memory.

FilmVault started as a simple question: why is it so hard to keep track of the films you love, the ones you want to see, and the ones you keep recommending to people?

Streaming apps bury your history. Letterboxd is great but web-first. FilmVault is a native Android app that puts your collection first — fast, local, and beautifully organised.
